FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How long does it take to dry?

Most carpets dry in a few hours depending on airflow, humidity, and soil level. Fans help a lot.

Do you move furniture?

Light furniture can be moved. For large items, we’ll clean around them unless you request otherwise.

Can you remove every stain?

Many stains come out, but some set-in stains may only lighten. We’ll be straight with you up front.

What should I do before you arrive?

Pick up small items, clear fragile décor, and point out problem spots. We’ll handle the rest.
